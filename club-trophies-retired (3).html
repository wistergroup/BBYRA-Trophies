Challenge Cup

Sneakbox Fleet — Manasquan River Yacht Club / BBYRA Race

The Challenge Cup is the legacy of a five-year series of annual three-race regattas between the Belmar Yacht Club and Manasquan River Yacht Club beginning in 1906, retired to MRYC after a tie-breaking regatta in 1910. The sterling silver trophy was tooled by Tiffany & Co., New York at a cost of $250. A base was added and the trophy used for an open Sneakbox regatta for many years. In 2003 it was rededicated to the winner of the Sneakbox fleet at the MRYC-hosted BBYRA regatta.
